Sourdough Bread Recipe

Photo by Frank Schrader

Description

Making sourdough bread might seem like a complex process due to the fact that making it does take up some time, but it actually is really simple once you get a strong sourdough starter. This self fermented bread not only tastes good but it's also better for your gut campared to othe types of bread. Once you master this basic recipe, you'll be able to make whatever flavor you can think of!
