Protein Baked Oats

Photo freshly ovened by Tracy Benjamin, CC BY-NC-ND 2.0

Description

These recipe is not only full of protein but it's also very delicious, contains no sugar, and my favorite part, you can even use only one dish!

Ingredients Needed

  • Ripe banana
  • Oats
  • Vanilla protein powder
  • Baking powder
  • Egg
  • Honey
  • Almond milk or water

Optional Ingredients

  • Blueberries
  • Greek yogurt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven

    2. Start by preheating your oven at 200Â°c for 10 minutes.

  2. Add ingredients

    3. In a small bowl, mash 1 ripe banana with a fork until there's no clumps. Add 1/2 cup of oats, 1 scoop of vanilla protein powder, 1 teaspoon of baking powder, 1 egg, 1 teaspoon of honey, and 1 cup of water or almond milk.

  3. Mix

    4. Mix all of the ingredients until combined.

  4. Add Toppings

    5. Pour your mixture into a small oven safe container. You can add any toppings you want, but if you'd like to get a blueberry cheesecake like dessert then I recommend adding 1/2 a cup of your favorite greek yogurt and as many blueberries as you wish on top of your mix.

  5. Bake

    6. Put your container in the oven for 45 minutes.

Enjoy!
