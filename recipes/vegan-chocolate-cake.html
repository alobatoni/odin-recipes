Vegan Chocolate Cake

Photo by Alexander Dummer

Description

Chocolate cake is extremely easy to make and very delicious! This vegan recipe will give you a very moist and rich in flavour dessert.

Check out the original recipe

Ingredients Needed

  • All purpose flour
  • White sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Salt
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Vegetable oil
  • Vanilla extract
  • Distilled white vinegar

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven

    2. Preheat your oven at 175°c and prepare your baking pan by greasing it with a little bit of oil.

  2. Mix flour and baking soda

    3. Grab a bowl and sift 1½ cups of all purpose flour and 1 teaspoon of baking soda.

  3. Prepare your chocolate mix

    4. In a separate bowl add 1 cup of water, 1 cup of white sugar, ¼ cup of cocoa powder, ½ teaspoon of salt and mix.

  4. Add the rest of your ingredients

    5. Into your chocolate mix add ⅓ cup of vegetable oil, 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract, 1 teaspoon of distilled white vinegar and mix everything.

  5. Prepare the batter

    6. Pour the wet mixture over the bowl containing flour and baking soda. With a silicon spatula, fold everything until there's no dry spots. Pour your batter onto the greased pan.

  6. Bake

    7. Insert your batter into the oven for 45 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean when inserted in the middle of the cake.

Enjoy!
